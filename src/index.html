About Us

Welcome to Cultiv8.

Here at Cultiv8, we are passionate about small
businesses who are dedicated to bringing fresh
food and products to their community. We realize
that there is a disconnect between local businesses
and consumers. Cultiv8 aims to solve this, by
creating a user experience centered on
cultivating the small business and
consumer relationship.

Get Involved

Interested in contributing?

Whether you want to volunteer your time for a
weekend, or bring an event to a farmer's market,
keep reading. Farmer's markets are only possible
because people come together to create them.
If you are interested in becoming involved, sign
up for Cultiv8, and select the farmer's markets
near you and find out exactly what they are
looking for.
